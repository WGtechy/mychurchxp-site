MyChurchXp Systems

Comprehensive Systems in one solution

Automated reporting

Reports of all scheduled meetings (Church services, Special meeting, Departmental meetings, Cells/Units/Fellowship meetings) are generated automatically.

Service Minister or Planners engagement

Your order of service can now be digital and easily managed. With MyChurchXp you can create a closed group for all your service ministers for easy follow of data, manage and distribute tasks to all service ministers/planners.

Members engagement

MyChurchXp software allows you and your members to socialize virtually, engaging and sharing their expectations, testimonies and experiences from church meetings. You can also collate data from a scheduled post-dated meetings from members. Also as the church pastor you can engage your members directly via chats and grant access to some members to do same or make it general

Department and units management

You can create and manage all your church departments and receive periodic meeting reports for assessment or review. Every department and units are to function in every church services if specified and as the church pastor, you will have full insight into their performance when it comes to participation.

Partnership and givings

Members can see all their giving records if the option is turned on. As the church pastor you have access to giving records and analytics at every meeting or any time a giving is made. You can also grant access right to some department if necessary.

Fully Secured

All data and features on this platform are only accessible by the Church Pastor. The Church Pastor can manually grant members and department/units access rights to view or modify resources.

Church Networking

If you have sub-churches, all your sub-churches can connect with yours to form a larger network of churches. You can have access to all the growth analytics, members and other resources if your sub-church Pastor grant you access.
